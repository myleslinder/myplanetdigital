Myplanet Participating in Startup Open House Toronto

Janna Hagan
  • community

Myplanet is honoured to be included in a collection of Toronto's finest startups. We are opening our doors on October 30th, 2014.

In an effort to open up our vibrant startup community, Myplanet is participating in Startup Open House Toronto, a event that is aimed at gaining more visibility amongst the startup community to the general public and to make some great connections in the process. With growing interest for working at startups and small businesses, the event wants to showcase what it's actually like to work at one.

You can learn more over at the official Startup Open House website.

Event Details

Date: October 30th, 2014
Time: 4pm - 7pm
Location: Myplanet 252 Adelaide Street East, Toronto, ON M5A 1N1
