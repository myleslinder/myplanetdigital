The Rules of the Telco Marketing Survey

The Telco Marketing Survey (the "Survey") is distributed by Myplanet Digital ("Myplanet").

Eligibility

Subject to the Rules (as defined herein) each Eligible Participant (as defined herein) who submits an Eligible Survey (as defined herein) is eligible to receive a Gift (as defined herein).

An "Eligible Participant" is an individual who satisfies the following criteria:

  • the individual is a legal resident of Canada (excluding residents of Quebec) who has reached the age of majority according to the law of the province in which he or she resides;

  • the individual is either a marketing manager, or other marketing professional, working in the telecommunications industry; and

  • upon completion of the Survey, the individual provides to Myplanet his or her: (A) name; (B) email address or phone number; and (C) link to a public LinkedIn profile, if any.

An "Eligible Survey" means a Survey completed in full by an Eligible Participant and submitted to

Myplanet in the manner, and by the date, if any, set out in the Survey and these Rules. For the avoidance of doubt, incomplete Surveys will be ineligible to receive any Gift, even if submitted by an Eligible Participant.

A participant may only complete and submit one (1) Survey. For the avoidance of doubt, there is a limit of one (1) entry per person.

1. Acceptance of Rules

The completion and submission to Myplanet of the Survey (whether an Eligible Survey or otherwise) by an individual (whether an Eligible Participant or otherwise) (each, a "Participant") constitutes such Participant's full and unconditional agreement to these Rules of the Survey (the "Rules"), as may be amended by Myplanet at its sole discretion. Receiving a Gift is contingent upon fulfilling all requirements set forth herein.

2. Gifts

Subject to Section 3(c), upon submission of an Eligible Survey by an Eligible Participant in accordance with the Rules, each Eligible Participant shall have the choice of one (1) of the following three (3) gifts (each, a "Gift"):

  • $75 Amazon gift certificate (the "Gift Certificate");

  • $75 donation to The Red Cross; (the "Donation" and together with the Gift Certificate, the "Restricted Gifts"); or

  • a report outlining the results of the Survey (the " Report").

Each Eligible Participant who submits an Eligible Survey will be notified by email or telephone of such

individual's entitlement to receive a Gift and the manner in which such Eligible Participant may retrieve

the Gift. Each such individual shall have fourteen (14) days from the date of receiving such notice

(the "Decision Period") to inform Myplanet in writing of his or her choice of Gift.

THERE IS A LIMITED NUMBER OF GIFT CERTIFICATES AND DONATIONS AVAILABLE. ONLY THE FIRST FIFTY (50) ELIGIBLE PARTICIPANTS THAT REQUEST A RESTRICTED GIFT WITHIN THEIR RESPECTIVE DECISION PERIODS MAY RECEIVE A RESTRICTED GIFT.

Once fifty (50) Restricted Gifts have been requested by Eligible Participants in accordance with Section 3(b), no other Eligible Participant may choose to receive a Restricted Gift. FOR THE AVOIDANCE OF DOUBT, NO MORE THAN FIFTY (50) GIFT CERTIFICATES AND DONATIONS IN THE AGGREGATE SHALL BE DISTRIBUTED IN CONNECTION WITH THE SURVEY. For greater certainty, subject to Section 3(a), each Eligible Participant may request the Report as his or her Gift.

For the avoidance of doubt, incomplete Surveys will be ineligible to receive any Gift, even if submitted by an Eligible Participant;

3. Terms of Acceptance

Each Gift must be accepted as awarded. No substitution, assignment or transfer of Gifts is permitted, except: (i) as otherwise explicitly provided herein; or (ii) at Myplanet's sole discretion. Myplanet reserves the right to substitute the Gift with one of comparable value or greater value without liability or consent. All costs and expenses not specifically included above as part of any gift, including, without limitation, any applicable taxes, are the sole responsibility of the recipient.

4. Privacy

Myplanet and its authorized agents and/or representatives may collect, use and disclose the personal information provided by each Participant at the time they submit the Survey for the purposes of: (i) administering the Survey and Gift fulfillment; and (ii) contacting the Participant via email or telephone to pose follow-up questions, if any. By submitting a Survey, each participant expressly consents to the use of their personal information for such purposes. For further information about Myplanet's privacy practices, please visit Myplanet's Privacy Policy here.

Myplanet any of their employees, any other Participants or any other person. Participants engaging in any of the foregoing activities may be disqualified and may accordingly forfeit any Gift received.

The decisions of Myplanet in regards to all matters of the Survey are final and binding upon Participants. In the event of any dispute regarding the interpretation of the Rules or any decision rendered by Myplanet, the decision or interpretation of Myplanet shall prevail.
