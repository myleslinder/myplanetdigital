Myplanet Sponsoring Women Who Code Toronto

Janna Hagan
  • community

On May 29th, Women Who Code Toronto will be hosting a Spring Social at Bitmaker Labs, sponsored by Myplanet.

Women Who Code is a global organization dedicated to inspiring women to pursue and excel in technology careers. WWC tripled in 2013, and now has nearly 10,000 members in 10 countries, with a new city being launched every week. WWC is known for its weekly technical study groups and larger monthly events including tech talks, hack nights, and career trainings.

As a proud supporter of women in technology, Myplanet has teamed up with Women Who Code Toronto as a sponsor for the Spring Social. All donations and proceeds from the raffle will go towards Autism Ontario, so that kids with autism can learn to code with the help of CoderDojoTO. By joining forces, we can all pitch in a little bit to strengthen the Toronto tech community.

Women Who Code Toronto was co-founded by Lidia Sienkowska and Bitmaker Labs.

To get your ticket please head to: http://www.eventbrite.ca/e/women-who-code-to-spring-social-tickets-11625637595 and join the WWCTO Meetup group here: http://www.meetup.com/Women-Who-Code-Toronto/events/182850962/.
