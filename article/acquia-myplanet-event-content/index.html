Myplanet and Acquia Host Digital Transformational Tour

Janna Hagan

Myplanet is proud to present the Digital Transformation Event in partnership with Acquia on October 28th, 2014.

Great digital experiences enable you to differentiate your business and delight your propsects and customers. Organizations today have a choice: evolve or die.

Trying to better satisfy and serve customers across web, mobile, commerce or social? Considering how digital tools help you disrupt your market growth? Are you just plain frustrated with your web content management system?

Here are just a few things you'll learn:

  • What digital transformation is and how top organizations are winning in the "Age of the Customer."
  • Why organizations often fail with web content management and digital experience technology.
  • How IT and Business can better work together.
  • Examples from world-class businesses and brands using Acquia's platform to transform, speed up digital work, and better serve their audiences.

On October 28th, Acquia and Myplanet are joining together to present an exclusive event for digital marketers and technology professionals. You'll be able to network with leading companies and get expert advice on how to jumpstart your web content management and digital transofrmational effort.

The presentation will be given by David Aponovich, who is the senior director of digital experience at Acquia. David was a senior analyst at Forrester Research, where he focused on web content management and digital experience platforms and consulted to Forrester's global clients on their WCM and digital transformation initiatives.
