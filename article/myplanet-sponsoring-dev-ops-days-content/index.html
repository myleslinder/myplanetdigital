Myplanet Sponsoring DevOps Days

Janna Hagan
  • community

DevOps Days is a community-driven, open-source event series taking place worldwide since 2009, bringing together the world’s DevOps enthusiasts to learn, discuss and develop the discipline.

This year for the first time ever, Toronto is hosting its first DevOps Days on September 12-13, 2014.

DevOps Days is for developers, operators, system architects, engineers and the merely curious to connect and share stories, advice, ideas and tools amongst people who care about collaboration, automation, culture and continuous improvement facets of DevOps practice.

This is an event that brings the community together to discuss and learn about key topics that support leading edge best practices and collaborative forums for industry peers. DevOps is an increasingly important mindset of product delivery operations and is a new philosophy to for enterprise organizations revolutionizing productivity and innovation on a global scale.

Myplanet is a proud Platinum sponsor for the first event of its kind in the city of Toronto and a strong supporter of Toronto’s DevOps enthusiast community.

Steve Pereira from Myplanet is directly involved in this community as the owner of Toronto’s monthly DevOps and OpenStack meetups, and the lead organizer of DevOps Days Toronto. He can be seen on Twitter @steveElsewhere curating DevOps-related content.
