More Than Lofty Promises

B Corporations are companies that voluntarily meet higher standards of transparency, accountability, and performance (as evaluated by the nonprofit B Lab). B Corporations believe that they can do well by doing good, benefiting society while demonstrating strong financial performance.

Certified B Corporations are rated on four Impact Areas:

  • Governance - Our mission, stakeholder engagement, and overall transparency of practices and policies.
  • Workers - Our relationship with our team. Assesses compensation, quality of work environment, communication, and culture.
  • Community - Our impact on our community. Includes supplier relations, diversity, and community involvement.
  • Environment - Our commitment to environmental best practices. Includes facilities, energy/materials use, and emissions.

Next Steps

While attaining Certified B Corporation status means we've committed to a higher standard than most, we believe this is just the beginning. Achieving 93/200 possible points means we have a long way to go to be the shining example we know we can be, but having an objective third-party measure of our progress means we know where we need to improve.

We hope to report back frequently on our progress as we continue to develop our company as a positive, contributing member of our community and society. Your suggestions and feedback are welcome!
